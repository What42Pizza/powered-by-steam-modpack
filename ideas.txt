Within 'Better Oil Refining':

- refineries have 4 output slots
- chemical plants have 3 output slots
- coke is a new fuel that lasts 4x longer than coal
- oil refining takes steam and crude oil as input and outputs hot tar, kerosene, petroleum, and volatile gas
- tar refining takes hot tar and sulfuric acid as input and outputs lubricating oil (lubricant but renamed), sulfuric acid (90% return rate), and coke
- tar cracking takes hot tar and sulfuric acid as input and outputs kerosene, volatile gas, sulfuric acid (90% return rate), and coke
- kerosene cracking takes kerosene and sulfuric acid as input and outputs petroleum, volatile gas, and sulfuric acid (90% return rate)
- sulfur is mined (just like iron/copper/coal etc)
- sulfuric acid is made with sulfur and steam and outputs stone and sulfuric acid
- solid fuel is made with volatile gas + petroleum (most efficient) or petroleum + kerosene
- rocket fuel is made with volatile gas + solid fuel
- asphalt (just another paver like concrete) can be made with hot tar and stone
- make flamethrowers only take crude oil and kerosene
